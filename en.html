About C-chan piano — Dragon Quest Piano Channel on YouTube

What you will find

The channel highlights Dragon Quest music through piano performance, making it easy to enjoy the emotional range of the series: adventure, nostalgia, calm town themes, and dramatic battle tracks.

Who this is for

It is a great fit for Dragon Quest fans, listeners who enjoy game music on piano, and anyone who wants a quiet but expressive way to revisit familiar themes.

Performance style and repertoire

Drawing on a classical piano background, the channel delivers carefully crafted Dragon Quest arrangements from DQ1 through DQ11. Field themes, battle tracks, endings, and medleys are all covered — from quiet background listening to focused soundtrack sessions.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the C-chan piano YouTube channel?

C-chan piano is a YouTube channel dedicated to Dragon Quest piano performances, covering DQ1 through DQ11 with playlists organized by series, medleys, and battle themes.

Which Dragon Quest games are covered?

The channel covers a wide range of titles from Dragon Quest I to Dragon Quest XI. Use the playlist filters on this page to browse by individual game title.

What is the difference between medley and series playlists?

Medley playlists combine tracks from multiple Dragon Quest titles into continuous listening sessions — great for background music. Series playlists focus on a single numbered title, letting you go deep into your favourite game's soundtrack.

Is the channel free to watch?

Yes, all performances on the YouTube channel are free to watch. Subscribing lets you stay updated with new uploads.
